<p><p>The fully automatic laser marking machine market includes various types, prominently optical fiber and carbon dioxide (CO2) lasers. Optical fiber lasers utilize a flexible fiber as the gain medium, offering high precision, efficiency, and versatility, suitable for metals and some plastics. In contrast, CO2 lasers employ a gas mixture for marking non-metal materials like wood, glass, and acrylic. Both types enhance production speed and quality in various industries, including manufacturing, packaging, and electronics, by ensuring durable markings and increased automation.</p></p>
