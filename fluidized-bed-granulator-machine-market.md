<p><p>The Fluidized Bed Granulator Machine market is segmented into wet granulation and dry granulation types. Wet granulation involves the addition of a liquid binder to particles, enhancing cohesion and ensuring uniform granule formation, often used in pharmaceuticals and food industries. In contrast, dry granulation utilizes mechanical force to compact powders without moisture, resulting in granules with high density and stable properties. Each method has distinct advantages, catering to different industrial requirements and product characteristics.</p></p>
