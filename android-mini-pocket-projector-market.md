<p><ul><li>DLP Technology</li><li>LCoS Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Android Mini Pocket Projector market encompasses two primary technologies: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors use micro mirrors to reflect light, ensuring bright and vibrant images, making them suitable for portable use. In contrast, LCoS projectors utilize liquid crystals on a reflective surface, offering higher resolution and superior color accuracy, often favored for home theaters. Both technologies cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing portability, image quality, and price points in the compact projector market.</p></p>
