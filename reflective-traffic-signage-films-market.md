<p><strong>The Reflective Traffic Signage Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microprismatic</li><li>Glass Beads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective Traffic Signage Films can be categorized into two primary types: Microprismatic and Glass Beads. Microprismatic films utilize advanced microprism technology to enhance reflectivity and visibility, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and nighttime conditions. They offer better performance under low light and adverse weather. In contrast, Glass Beads films incorporate tiny glass beads embedded in the film to provide reflectivity. While generally more cost-effective, they may not offer the same level of brightness or durability as microprismatic options.</p></p>
