<p><p>Evaporation coating machines are utilized in various industries for applying thin films on substrates. The two primary market types are Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves vaporizing materials in a vacuum to achieve a coating, suitable for electronics and optics. In contrast, CVD utilizes chemical reactions to deposit materials at the molecular level, ideal for producing high-performance coatings in semiconductors and solar cells. Both methods offer unique benefits, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
