<p><ul><li>Evaporating Style Heat Cost Allocator</li><li>Electric Heat Cost Allocator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Heat Metering System market includes two primary types: Evaporating Style Heat Cost Allocators and Electric Heat Cost Allocators. Evaporating Style allocators measure heat consumption based on the evaporation of a liquid, providing accurate data for individual units within a building. In contrast, Electric Heat Cost Allocators utilize electrical energy to assess heating usage, offering real-time monitoring. Both systems promote energy efficiency by enabling precise billing and incentivizing residents to conserve heat, thus supporting overall energy management strategies.</p></p>
