<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market consists of two primary methods: fermentation-limited and dealcoholization. The fermentation-limited method involves restricting fermentation to ensure minimal alcohol production, retaining the beer's flavors while keeping alcohol content low. In contrast, the dealcoholization method removes alcohol from fully brewed beer, often through techniques like vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods cater to consumers seeking healthier beverage options, offering a variety of flavors that mimic traditional beer without the intoxicating effects of alcohol.</p></p>
