<p><p>The sealing coatings market is divided into two primary types: liquid sealing coatings and dry sealing coatings. Liquid sealing coatings are comprised of fluid formulations that create a protective layer upon application, offering flexibility and easy coverage for various surfaces. In contrast, dry sealing coatings consist of solid or powder forms that, once applied, solidify to form a durable barrier. Both types serve to enhance surface protection, improve adhesion, and extend the lifespan of materials in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
