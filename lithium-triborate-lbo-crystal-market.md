<p><p>Lithium triborate (LBO) crystals are widely utilized in nonlinear optics for applications such as second harmonic generation (SHG) and sum frequency generation (SFG). In SHG, LBO converts fundamental wavelengths into their harmonics, facilitating the generation of new frequencies for laser applications. SFG combines two wavelengths to create a new frequency, enhancing spectroscopy and imaging techniques. Additionally, LBO is used in other markets like frequency mixing and optical parametric oscillation, contributing to advancements in laser technology and material processing.</p></p>
