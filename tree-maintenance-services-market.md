<p><p>Tree maintenance services encompass two primary market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing issues after they occur, such as tree removals, pruning, or disease treatment to rectify safety hazards or aesthetic concerns. In contrast, preventive maintenance focuses on proactive measures to maintain tree health and reduce future problems, including regular inspections, pruning schedules, and fertilization. Both approaches are essential for ensuring the safety and longevity of trees in urban and rural environments.</p></p>
