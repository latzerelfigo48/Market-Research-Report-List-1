<p><ul><li>PE-Xa</li><li>PE-Xb</li><li>PE-Xc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) pipe is categorized into three types based on the crosslinking method: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through the peroxide method, offering superior flexibility and heat resistance. PE-Xb utilizes the silane method, providing good mechanical properties at a lower cost. PE-Xc employs electron beam irradiation for crosslinking, resulting in enhanced thermal stability. These variations cater to diverse applications in plumbing, heating, and industrial systems, driving their market growth.</p></p>
