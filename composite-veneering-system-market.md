<p><ul><li>Direct Composite Veneers</li><li>Indirect Composite Veneers</li><li>Prefabricated Veneers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Composite Veneering System market comprises three main types: Direct Composite Veneers, Indirect Composite Veneers, and Prefabricated Veneers. </p><p>Direct Composite Veneers are applied directly to the teeth in a single appointment, allowing for quick cosmetic adjustments. Indirect Composite Veneers involve creating custom veneers in a lab, which are then bonded to the teeth, offering enhanced aesthetics and durability. Prefabricated Veneers are pre-made shells that can be easily adapted to patients' teeth, providing a cost-effective and time-saving solution without custom fabrication.</p></p>
