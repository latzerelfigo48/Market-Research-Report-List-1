<p><strong>The Wafer Polishing Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pads</li><li>Post CMP Cleaning Solution</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer polishing materials market comprises several key types: CMP slurry, CMP pads, post-CMP cleaning solutions, and others. CMP slurry consists of abrasive particles suspended in a liquid, crucial for removing material during the chemical mechanical polishing process. CMP pads provide a surface for the wafer to be polished against, enabling uniform contact. Post-CMP cleaning solutions are used to remove residues left after polishing, ensuring wafer cleanliness. Other materials may include additional polishing compounds and equipment accessories that enhance the CMP process.</p></p>
