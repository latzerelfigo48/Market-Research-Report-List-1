<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impregnation resins are classified into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I resins are primarily used for electrical insulation and moisture protection in applications like transformers and capacitors due to their superior dielectric properties. Type II resins, on the other hand, are designed for structural applications, providing enhanced mechanical strength and thermal stability, often utilized in automotive and aerospace sectors. Both types cater to specific industry needs, influencing their formulations and performance characteristics.</p></p>
