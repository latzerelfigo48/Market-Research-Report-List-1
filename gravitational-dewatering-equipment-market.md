<p><strong>The Gravitational Dewatering Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Gravity Dehydration</li><li>Gravity Concentration Dehydration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gravitational dewatering equipment encompasses methods for enhancing the separation of water from solids through gravity-based processes. Natural gravity dehydration utilizes the natural inclination of water to separate from solid materials without mechanical intervention, ideal for low-energy applications. In contrast, gravity concentration dewatering involves techniques that leverage gravitational forces to enhance the separation efficiency, often used in mineral processing. Both methods are crucial in industries like mining and wastewater treatment, improving resource recovery and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
