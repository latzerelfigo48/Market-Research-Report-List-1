<p><p>The electro-diagnostic devices market encompasses various technologies used to assess electrical activity in different body systems. </p><p>Electromyogram (EMG) evaluates muscle function by measuring electrical signals; Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS) assess nerve health by determining signal speed. Electroretinography (ERG) evaluates retinal function, and Electrogastrography (EGG) assesses gastric motility. Together, these modalities aid in diagnosing neurological, cardiac, and gastrointestinal disorders, contributing to advancements in patient care and treatment strategies.</p></p>
