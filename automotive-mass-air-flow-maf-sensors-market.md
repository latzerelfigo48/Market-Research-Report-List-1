<p><ul><li>Hot Wire</li><li>Hot Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Hot Wire and Hot Film sensors. Hot Wire sensors utilize a heated wire element to measure airflow, with resistance changes indicating air volume. They offer high accuracy and rapid response times. Hot Film sensors, on the other hand, employ a thin film sensor that detects changes in temperature as air passes over it, providing similar functionality but with enhanced stability and durability. Both types are essential for optimizing engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
