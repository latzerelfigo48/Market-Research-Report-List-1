<p><p>The aerospace propellant market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Solid Propellant**: Comprised of a mixture of fuel and oxidizer in a solid form, offering simplicity and reliability, often used in missiles and space launch vehicles.</p><p>2. **Liquid Propellant**: Consisting of separate fuel and oxidizer stored in liquid form, providing greater control over thrust and enabling multiple engine starts, commonly used in rockets.</p><p>3. **Gaseous Propellant**: Utilizing gas for propulsion, often employed in specific high-temperature applications or as reaction control systems.</p><p>4. **Other Propellants**: Includes hybrid and developmental propellants, combining aspects of the above types for innovative applications in aerospace technology.</p></p>
