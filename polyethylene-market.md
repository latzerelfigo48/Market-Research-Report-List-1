<p><p>The polyethylene market is categorized into several types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its strong, rigid properties, making it ideal for containers and pipes. LDPE is more flexible and lightweight, often used in plastic bags and films. LLDPE, characterized by its stretchability and toughness, is commonly used for packaging materials. Other types may include special grades or blends, expanding the applications across various industries.</p></p>
