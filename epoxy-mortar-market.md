<p><p>The epoxy mortar market primarily consists of two types: two-component and three-component epoxy mortars. Two-component epoxy mortars are comprised of a resin and a hardener, mixed prior to application, offering strong adhesion and chemical resistance. Three-component epoxy mortars incorporate an additional aggregate, enhancing mechanical properties and durability for demanding applications. Both types are utilized in construction, flooring, and repair works, providing superior bonding strength and resilience, thereby catering to various industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
