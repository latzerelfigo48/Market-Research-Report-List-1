<p><p>BOPP laminating membranes are categorized primarily into two types: common type and heated adhesive type. The common type utilizes a standard adhesive layer for bonding, making it suitable for general applications like packaging and stationery. In contrast, the heated adhesive type employs heat-activated adhesives, which enhance bonding strength and durability, making it ideal for high-performance applications such as book covers and premium packaging. Both types offer protective qualities, enhancing durability and visual appeal in various uses.</p></p>
