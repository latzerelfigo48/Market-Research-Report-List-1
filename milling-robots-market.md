<p><p>Milling robots are categorized based on their axis configuration, which defines their movement capabilities. **4-axis robots** offer basic milling operations, suitable for tasks requiring linear movement and simple rotations. **5-axis robots** add an extra rotation axis, allowing more complex geometries and improved tool angles. **6-axis robots** enhance flexibility and can perform intricate milling tasks in three-dimensional space. **7-axis robots** provide even greater maneuverability, enabling access to difficult spaces and intricate part orientations. **Other** market solutions may include specialized milling robots tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
