The market includes the Lens Shift Method, which compensates for camera shake by shifting the lens itself, allowing for real-time corrections. Conversely, the Module Tilt Method stabilizes the camera module's orientation to counteract motion. Both methods enhance image quality, catering to various consumer needs and technological advances in imaging devices.</p></p>
