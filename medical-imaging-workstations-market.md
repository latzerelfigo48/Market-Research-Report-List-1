<p><p>Medical imaging workstations are essential tools for radiologists and healthcare professionals, categorized into thin client and thick client workstations. Thin client workstations rely on centralized servers for processing and storage, allowing for easier maintenance and remote access, which enhances collaboration. In contrast, thick client workstations offer more robust local processing power and storage capabilities, making them suitable for handling complex imaging tasks directly on-site. Both types play vital roles in optimizing diagnostic workflows and improving patient care.</p></p>
