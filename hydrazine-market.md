<p><ul><li>Ultra-Pure Hydrazine</li><li>Anhydrous Hydrazine</li><li>Hydrazine hydrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrazine market consists of three primary types: Ultra-Pure Hydrazine, Anhydrous Hydrazine, and Hydrazine Hydrate. Ultra-Pure Hydrazine is characterized by its high purity and is essential for applications in aerospace and pharmaceuticals. Anhydrous Hydrazine, devoid of water, is mainly used as a rocket propellant and in various chemical syntheses. Hydrazine Hydrate, a solution of hydrazine in water, is commonly employed in agriculture, water treatment, and as a reducing agent in chemical processes. Each type serves distinct industrial purposes.</p></p>
