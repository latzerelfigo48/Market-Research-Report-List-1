<p><p>The metalworking treating fluid market is primarily divided into semi-synthetic and synthetic fluids. Semi-synthetic fluids are a blend of both mineral oil and synthetic components, offering good lubrication and cooling properties while reducing harmful residue. They are versatile and suitable for various machining processes. Synthetic fluids, on the other hand, do not contain mineral oils and are composed entirely of chemical compounds, providing superior cooling and lubrication. They are often more temperature resistant and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
