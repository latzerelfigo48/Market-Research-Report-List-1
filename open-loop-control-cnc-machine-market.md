<p><p>The open-loop control CNC machine market comprises three key types: microcontroller-based, motion control chip-based, and DSP-based systems. Microcontroller-based CNC machines utilize standard microcontrollers for basic command execution and process control, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Motion control chip-based systems incorporate specialized chips that enhance performance and precision, suited for more complex applications. DSP-based CNC machines leverage digital signal processing technology for advanced trajectory planning and real-time control, delivering high-speed and accurate operations ideal for intricate manufacturing tasks.</p></p>
