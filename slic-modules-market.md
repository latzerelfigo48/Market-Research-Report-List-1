<p><p>SLIC (Single-Line Interface Circuit) modules are essential in telecommunications for connecting phone lines to digital systems. The market for these modules is categorized into two types: single-voltage and dual-voltage. Single-voltage SLIC modules operate at a single voltage level, simplifying design and reducing costs. In contrast, dual-voltage SLIC modules can operate at two different voltage levels, providing flexibility for various applications and ensuring compatibility with diverse systems. This differentiation influences market demand based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
