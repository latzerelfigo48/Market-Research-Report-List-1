<p><strong>The Electro Absorption Modulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Modulators</li><li>Refractive Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro Absorption Modulators (EAMs) are devices that manipulate light signals for communication applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: absorptive modulators and refractive modulators. Absorptive modulators operate by altering the light absorption properties of a material in response to an electric field, allowing for efficient signal modulation. In contrast, refractive modulators change the refractive index of a material, affecting how light passes through it. Both types are vital for high-speed data transmission in optical networks.</p></p>
